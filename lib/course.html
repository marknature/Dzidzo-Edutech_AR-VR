Also Visit Our AR Library

The Dzidzo Virtual Library is a quiet, immersive space designed for exploration, reading, and reflection. Filled with digital books, interactive learning materials, and AR-powered visual aids, it offers students a unique way to engage with content beyond the classroom. Whether you're revising for exams, exploring African history, or diving into science concepts, the library brings knowledge to life—one virtual shelf at a time.

Immersive Reading Experience

Step into a digital environment where books come alive through AR and interactive visuals, making complex topics easier to understand and more engaging.

Culturally Relevant Content

Access a growing collection of materials that reflect African history, languages, and perspectives—curated to support learners in local contexts.

Accessible Anytime, Anywhere

No need for physical space or printed books. The Dzidzo Library is available 24/7 from any device with a browser—empowering self-paced, independent learning.

Dzidzo

Dzidzo is an open-source AR/VR-powered education platform designed to make immersive learning accessible to everyone—especially in underserved communities across Africa. By combining modern web development with interactive 3D and augmented reality technologies, Dzidzo brings textbooks to life and transforms classrooms into engaging digital environments. Our mission is to empower students and educators with tools that are not only innovative but also affordable, culturally relevant, and easy to use on any device.
