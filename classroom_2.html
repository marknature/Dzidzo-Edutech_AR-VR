This one-of-a-kind outdoor classroom isn't just a digital space—it's a vision of what education can become. Created by Nature, a passionate technologist and the lead architect of Dzidzo, this immersive metaclassroom blends the beauty of nature with the power of virtual learning. Inspired by his deep love for the outdoors and his commitment to accessible education, Mark designed this environment to bring students out of the traditional box and into an open world of curiosity, wonder, and connection.

The future of learning isn't confined to four walls—and thanks to creators like Mark, it's now rooted in nature and delivered through the metaverse. Wherever you are, you're invited to step in, learn, and grow.

Dzidzo

Dzidzo is an open-source AR/VR-powered education platform designed to make immersive learning accessible to everyone—especially in underserved communities across Africa. By combining modern web development with interactive 3D and augmented reality technologies, Dzidzo brings textbooks to life and transforms classrooms into engaging digital environments. Our mission is to empower students and educators with tools that are not only innovative but also affordable, culturally relevant, and easy to use on any device.
